comfort_temp_c limit_temp_c extreme_temp_c fill_type fill_power
Show 3 parameter details
comfort_temp_c — temperature at which average person sleeps comfortably
Numeric: temperature in °C (e.g., "13" for 13°C comfort)
fill_type — insulation material (maps to Goal #6 P9)
down · goose-down · synthetic · hollow-fiber · baf-micro · bhb-micro · primaloft

🏷 D1 — Product Identity

The Zulu Ultralight 700 is a summer-weight mummy sleeping bag with Warmthal microfiber insulation, rated to 13°C comfort. At 780g and packing down to 27×13 cm, it is designed for weight-conscious cyclists, bikepackers, and festival-goers who need minimal pack volume. The Polyester Micro Ripstop shell provides moisture resistance while keeping weight under 800g. At 999 Kč, it's the most affordable and lightest option in this comparison.

🎯 D2 — Ideal For

  • ● Summer cycling and bikepacking — at 780g and 27×13 cm packed, fits easily into a pannier or frame bag
  • ● Festival camping and hostels — lightweight, affordable, hypoallergenic synthetic fill for casual overnight stays
  • ● Motorcycle touring — compact enough for saddlebag storage, quick-drying Warmthal fill handles road-trip conditions
  • ● Warm-weather trekking — comfort rated to 13°C for summer mountain huts and lowland camping

⚙️ D3 — How It Works
